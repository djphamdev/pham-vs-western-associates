Core Allegation (FEHA §12940)

"Japanese employees, including Ayako, were held to materially different workplace standards than similarly situated American employees, resulting in disparate workload expectations, compensation disparities, heightened scrutiny, and significant emotional distress."

  • Only two employees in this role at SSS: Ayako (Japanese) and Maria (American).
  • Ayako generated significantly higher profit but was paid less.
  • Ayako was subjected to intense scrutiny, weekend/holiday work, and aggressive micromanagement by Japanese management (Yutaka Chiba).

Why This Case is Actionable Now

  • Right-to-Sue Obtained: Administrative phase complete.
  • Imminent Closure Risk: Southern Star Services is expected to close around June 2026 (lost major contract). Immediate litigation/discovery preservation is urgent.
  • Joint Employer Theory: Western Associates Inc. (Japan) exerted operational control over the California entity.
  • 245+ Evidentiary Items: Emails, LINE messages, timesheets, and performance records are pre-assembled and cataloged.
