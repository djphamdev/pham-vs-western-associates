Key Allegations

  • National origin discrimination (FEHA §12940) — Japanese national treated worse than American comparator

  • Disparate scrutiny of timekeeping and attendance

  • Failure to investigate or remediate after multiple internal complaints

Why This Case May Be Significant

  • Strong comparator evidence — direct side-by-side with same-role American employee
  • 200+ documented evidence items across 4+ years (2021-2025)
