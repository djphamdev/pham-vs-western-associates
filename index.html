Core Allegation (FEHA §12940)

"Ayako (Japanese National) was held to materially different workplace standards than the only other employee functioning in her role, Maria (American), resulting in disparate workload expectations, compensation disparities, heightened scrutiny, and significant emotional distress."

  • Ayako was hired as an Accountant but forced to absorb all VN099 (Vietnam Airlines) reservations and export duties with more responsibility but no title change.Ayako generated significantly higher profit/revenue but was paid significantly less.
  • Ayako was subjected to intense scrutiny, weekend/holiday work, and aggressive micromanagement by Japanese management, while Maria was allowed flexible standards.

Why This Case is Actionable Now

  • Right-to-Sue Obtained: Administrative phase complete.
  • Reactive Compliance: The Nov 2025 CRD filing prompted Southern Star Services to create its first-ever Employee Handbook, demonstrating the issues were real and required immediate formalization.
  • Imminent Closure Risk: Southern Star Services is expected to close around June 2026. Immediate litigation/discovery preservation is urgent.Joint Employer Theory: Western Associates Inc. (Japan) exerted operational control over the California entity.
  • 245+ Evidentiary Items: Emails, LINE messages, timesheets, and performance records are pre-assembled and cataloged.
